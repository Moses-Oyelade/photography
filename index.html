Biography of this webpage

Posted on May 14, 2023

Findings carried out over the years reveal that when we are inquisitive about the idea of nature and everything around us, we have little or no choice than explore history. History helps with divers of information that build up our ideology into encoumpassing knowledge about ethics of life. The Photography aspect of retaining our historical value can not be over emphasized, and this inspired this webpage PHOTOGRAPHY HUB.

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film, to create an image. With the right camera equipment, you can even photograph wavelengths of light invisible to the human eye, including UV, infrared, and radio. The first permanent photograph was captured in 1826 (some sources say 1827) by Joseph Nicéphore Niépce in France
